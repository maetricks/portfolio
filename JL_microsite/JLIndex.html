Who's Speaking?.

Travis Neilson

Youtuber / DevTips

Travis is trying to grow up, but instead spends most of his time making stuff and ignoring grown-up things like health insurance and shaving. He was raised in the desert streets of Las Vegas, but currently resides in Silicon Valley and finds pleasure in being able to Skateboard to work. When not at Google offices creating the internet, he is most likely with his family telling jokes that are only sometimes funny.

Tad Carpenter

Freelance Designer

Tad Carpenter is a designer, illustrator, author and teacher based in Kansas City, Missouri. Carpenter's studio, Tad Carpenter Creative’s overall approach to branding, strategy, and design have gained wonderful clients ranging from Target, Macy’s, Rayban, Old Navy, Nick Jr, Conan O'Brien, MTV and Adobe. Tad’s work has appeared in numerous publications and he has written/illustrated over a dozen children’s books in the market today. Since 2009, Tad has taught Graphic Design at the University of Kansas.

Liz Clements

Illustrator

Liz Clements is a freelance artist/illustrator based in London best known for her stylized collection of tattoo inspired art + design. Liz dedicates her time to preparing detailed doodles of tattooed babes, working in soft pencils, Clements finds space for all types of tattoos on the bodies she illustrates, from classic Americana to Japanese.

Tiphanie Brooke

Designer / Antigirl

Tiphanie Brooke is a rigorous problem solver, form maker, artist and award winning graphic designer. Her repertoire includes prototyping - merging art, design, and science. In addition to: screen printing, photography, typography, print making, illustration and painting. She has a seamless way of blending things she constructs by hand with things she puts together on screen, making it difficult to distinguish what has been done manually or through various imaging applications.
