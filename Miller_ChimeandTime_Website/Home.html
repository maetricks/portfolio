    Welcome to Chimes & Times

    Fine clock and watch repair since 1967 Chimes And Times provides quality clock and watch repairs to customers in Omaha, NE and the surrounding areas. For over 45 years we have been restoring your treasured timepieces to keep the memories within alive and ticking. We promise to use our expertise to make your timepiece look and work better than new.

    In order to keep this promise, Chimes And Times combines modern technology with time-honored craftsmanship. We will painstakingly repair, test and then re-test your timepiece to ensure the pest possible results. Our specialties are grandfather, grandmother and antique clocks.

    Chimes And Times also buys and sells a variety of clocks and watches. If you have one gathering dust, bring it to us and turn it into cash. If you are looking for a special, one-of-a-kind timepiece, come see us and one of our friendly staff will help you find the perfect item.

    Trust Chimes And Times for expert clock and watch repair at reasonable prices. Contact us for more information, or to obtain a free estimate on our services. We look forward to hearing from you!
